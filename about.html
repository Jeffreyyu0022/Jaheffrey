About

HELLO!! I'm Jeffrey Yu, a college student at the University of Southern California pursuing a degree in Computer Science with a focus on Game Development. What I find most exciting about programming is the ability to create interactive experiences that can bring together a community of people that might not have otherwise met.

To be completely transparent, what originally pulled me into programming over design, art, or sound was that I was good at it. It was a source of comfort, but now I find myself enjoying it more and more as I learn new things and apply them to my own projects. I get to integrate all of these amazing ideas and deliverables into one.

Outside of creating games, it should be no surprise that I enjoy playing them as well. Although this might be controversial, my current favorite game is "Valorant." I've always been competitive, and I love shooters, so this game was a perfect fit for me. Besides that, I also enjoy playing story games like "What Remains of Edith Finch" and multiplayer party games like "Meccha Chameleon!" Outside of games, I enjoy doing bodyweight exercises, going on runs or walks, and a new hobby of mine: bouldering!

P.S. Also hanging out with my girlfriend.
